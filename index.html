Data Analysis Reveals Critical Patterns

🔧 Vibration Analysis
Strong correlations identified between engine abnormalities and maintenance needs. Early vibration detection can predict failures 2-3 weeks before critical incidents.
🌦️ Weather Impact
Maintenance costs increase 40% during harsh weather conditions. Predictive scheduling based on weather forecasts can optimize maintenance timing.
✈️ Altitude Effects
High-altitude flights accelerate engine wear patterns significantly faster than current maintenance schedules account for, requiring data-driven adjustments.

These findings demonstrate clear opportunities for predictive maintenance to reduce costs and improve reliability.
